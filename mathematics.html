Welcome to the Maths Course

This course covers a wide range of mathematical concepts, from basic algebra to advanced calculus and linear algebra. We aim to enhance your problem-solving skills and understanding of mathematical theory through practical exercises and examples.

Algebra

Learn about variables, equations, and functions. Master techniques for solving linear and quadratic equations, simplifying expressions, and understanding functions.

Calculus

Dive into limits, derivatives, and integrals. Discover how calculus is used to model and understand changes in real-world scenarios.

Linear Algebra

Explore matrices, vectors, and systems of linear equations. Learn how linear algebra is used in computer science, engineering, and physics.

Probability and Statistics

Understand probability theory and statistical methods. Learn about random variables, distributions, and hypothesis testing.
