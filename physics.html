Welcome to the Physics Course

This course will help you understand the principles of mechanics, thermodynamics, and electromagnetism, along with the wonders of modern physics. We combine theoretical lessons with hands-on experiments to make complex concepts simple and fun!

Classical Mechanics

Study the laws of motion, gravity, and energy conservation to understand how objects move and interact under various forces.

Electromagnetism

Explore electric fields, magnetic fields, and the relationship between them through Maxwell's equations and applications like circuits and motors.

Quantum Physics

Discover the strange world of atoms and subatomic particles, where classical laws of physics don't apply.

Thermodynamics

Understand heat, temperature, and energy transformations, including the laws of thermodynamics that govern every process in nature.
